entity: Something that exists as a distinct, independent thing.
physical entity: Something that has a physical, material existence.
abstraction: A general idea or concept formed from specific examples.
abstract entity: An idea or concept that exists only in thought, not physically.
thing: Any object, matter, or concern that can be referred to or considered.
object: A physical item that can be seen and touched.
physical object: A material item that occupies space and can be perceived.
whole: A complete thing with all its parts included.
unit: A single, complete thing regarded as one element.
congener: Something of the same kind, origin, or category as another.
